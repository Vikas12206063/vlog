About Us

Energy Wastage by Gadgets is a non-profit organization dedicated to promoting energy conservation and reducing carbon emissions caused by gadgets. We believe that technology should not come at the expense of our planet, and that every individual can make a difference in preserving our environment.

Our Mission

Our mission is to raise awareness about the impact of energy wastage caused by gadgets and provide practical solutions for reducing energy consumption. We believe that education is the key to change, and we strive to empower individuals and communities to make sustainable choices.
