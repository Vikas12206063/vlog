Welcome to Energy Wastage by Gadgets! Our mission is to raise awareness about the impact of energy wastage caused by gadgets and provide practical solutions for reducing energy consumption.

Did you know that even when your gadgets are not in use, they are still consuming energy in standby mode? Or that charging your devices overnight wastes energy? These are just a few of the many ways gadgets contribute to energy wastage.

By taking simple steps to reduce your energy consumption, such as unplugging your gadgets when not in use or choosing energy-efficient devices, you can help reduce your carbon footprint and make a positive impact on the environment.
